What Do We Offer?

Our goal is to To elevate every event with exceptional bar catering experiences, crafting personalized cocktails and providing outstanding service that brings people together. Company Owner Dan Warnica says:

‘After working 50+ weddings in various roles, I determined that the most fun you can have is behind the bar. There is a chance to interact with every guest and you get to assess what is needed for the event to be a homerun. The value of a happy group is exactly what hospitality runs on, and exactly what I try to achieve with every event.’

What Services Do We Provide?

Mobile Bar Service

Indoor or outdoor pop-up bar for full cocktail service. Including weddings. Up to 200 guests. Please contact for packages and pricing.

Champagne Wall

Indoor or outdoor champagne wall with greenery for rent. Often accompanied by our signature mimosa bar where guests can select from our freshly squeezed fruit juice to pair with their champagne!

Cocktail Masterclass

Learn the art of cocktail creation for your next dinner party. Groups of up to 30 will be coached through making a list of classic cocktails, building up to mixing a signature creation for the group to try
